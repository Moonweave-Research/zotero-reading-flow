Beyond 3D Graphs: What features add the most value?

Since we are dropping the heavy 3D visualizations, we can focus on making the core reading and organizing experience incredibly smooth. Here are 3 feature packages we could focus on.

A

The Automated Tracker (Minimal Effort)

Focuses on eliminating manual updates. The plugin watches what you do and updates the list automatically.

  • Auto-Progress: Syncs the progress bar automatically based on how far you've scrolled in the PDF reader.
  • Auto-Rating: Suggests a 1-5 star rating based on your annotation density (how much you highlighted).
  • Dynamic Colors: Rows subtly shift from gray (unread) to green (finished) automatically.
B

The Visual Organizer (Maximum Control)

Focuses on giving you beautiful, manual control over how your library looks.

  • Custom Badges: Dedicated visual columns for "Priority" (🔥), "Difficulty" (🧠), or "Status" (✅).
  • Row Highlighting: Manually paint entire rows with custom colors so critical papers jump out at you.
  • Tag Colors: Enhanced tag rendering directly inside the list columns.
C

The Review Assistant (Quick Recall)

Focuses on helping you remember what a paper was about without having to open it.

  • Quick Peek: Hover over a paper in the list to see a popover with the abstract and your top 3 highlights.
  • Reading Time: A column that estimates how long the paper will take to read based on page count.
  • Highlight Sync: Automatically pulls your PDF highlights into a beautifully formatted Zotero Note.

* You can select multiple options if you want a combination!
