Final Design Review: Zotero 9 "Reading Flow" Plugin

Here is the consolidated design for our new, lightweight, high-impact plugin.

1. Core Features (The "What")

  • Smart Progress Column: A native-looking column showing reading progress. Auto-syncs with the built-in PDF reader.
  • Color & Badge System: Right-click a paper to paint the row a specific color or assign a status badge (🔥, ⏳, ✅).
  • Quick Peek Popover: Hover over any item to instantly read its Abstract and your top 3 annotations.

2. Technical Approach (The "How")

  • Zotero 9 Native: We will strictly use the new HTML/React-based UI API (no more hacky XUL overlays). This ensures it won't break on every Zotero update.
  • Zero Heavy Dependencies: By dropping 3D/WebGL, the plugin will load instantly and consume minimal RAM.
  • Data Storage: Store progress and color data safely in Zotero's built-in extra field or native item data so it syncs across devices seamlessly.
